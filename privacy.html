Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Letters In is a word game operated by RobTechUK. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Gameplay data such as scores and progress
  • Basic technical data (browser type, device, IP address)
  • Analytics data to understand how the game is used

2. Cookies and Local Storage

Letters In may use browser storage (such as localStorage) to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Store scores and preferences
  • Improve your experience

This data is stored on your device and is not shared unless you create an account.

3. Analytics

We may use tools such as Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the site. These tools may collect anonymised usage data.

4. Accounts (Future Feature)

If you choose to create an account in the future, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Leaderboard data (scores, rankings)

This information will only be used to provide game features such as login and leaderboards.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Run and improve the game
  • Maintain leaderboards
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with trusted services (such as hosting or analytics providers) where necessary.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
